Introduction

  • Local tax authorities are battling with non-
    compliance of holders of digital assets with
    regards to disclosure and reporting their tax
    obligations.

    This is a big loss of revenue for local tax
    authorities and it has a significant impact on
    the economy.

    On the other hand, crypto holders are confused
    as to how to handle the calculating their tax
    obligations as there is not clear guidelines
    from tax authorities.

    As the DLT, we believe that we can play a
    significant role in assisting our local tax
    authorities, the financial regulator and a
    significant number of cryptocurrency investors
    out there with regards to the compliance of
    digital assets.

    Cryptaxly is a product that we are building within
    DLT research team as proof of concept to assist
    individual and entities who holds massive amounts
    of digital assets to be tax-compliant.

The Product Offers:

  • • A dashboard to get birds eyes of portfolio performance
  • • A way to allow investors to correctly classify and reconcile transactions for correct tax reporting
  • • A tax calculator that generate tax reports from their income and losses

General Use Cases

  • Ensuring compliance with tax laws and regulations related to cryptocurrency transactions.
  • Helping individuals and businesses accurately calculate their tax liability related to cryptocurrency activities, such as buying, selling, and trading.
  • Reducing the risk of audit, penalties, and fines by maintaining accurate records and reporting cryptocurrency transactions properly.
  • Facilitating tax reporting and payment for individuals and businesses engaged in cryptocurrency activities.
  • Providing transparency and accountability in the use of cryptocurrency as a financial instrument, which can help prevent illicit activities such as money laundering and terrorist financing.

Digital Assets - Tax Compliance

Crypto tax compliance can help regulators increase revenue, promote fairness, prevent criminal activity, and regulate the crypto industry
