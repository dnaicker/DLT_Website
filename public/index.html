Our projects

Self-Sovereign Identity

Preserve privacy of user by providing a way to identity themselves without divulging confidential details to a third-party service using zero knowledge proofs and credentials.

Increased Trustworthiness of Product Quality

Track and tracing of transactions within a network system to evaluate fraudulent activities will help increase reliability and trustworthiness of a service for early-adopter and professional users.

Reduced Centralization of Compliance and Regulation Checks

Utilisation of smart contracts in blockchain technology can assure that local service regulations and policies implemented between revenue service and citizen are adhered to.

Metaverse

Distributed ledger technology would be utilized to connect to the Metaverse ecosystem by advancing aspects of security involved with accountability of transactions, independent of centralized restrictions and control. It underlies the underpinning of trust within a future social network away from censorship and making data truly owned by one’s own self.
