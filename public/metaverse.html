Metaverse - Introduction

  • Metaverse is a combination of existing and new technologies to create a virtual world in which users can communicate with others and interact with virtual environments.
  • There is a range of technologies that make up the Metaverse, from Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) to Blockchain and NFTs.
  • Augmented Reality (AR) : characterized by merging our reality with the digital world. Typically, this means adding digital elements to real-world settings.
  • Virtual Reality (VR) : characterized by a virtual world that continues to exist even when you are not immersed in it.
  • Crpto and NFTs : Cryptocurrency and especially NFTs (Non-fungible Tokens) play a big role within the Metaverse.
  • Avatars annd 3D Design: 3D design and avatars form the foundation of what is considered to be a Metaverse. If a Metaverse is to be explored, or 'lived within', then these environments must be designed.
